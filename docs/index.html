By using this site, you agree to the following:

1. Respectful Use -- Please speak kindly and respectfully, just as you would in church.
Do not use offensive, hateful, or inappropriate language.
Do not try to trick or confuse the chatbot into saying things it shouldn't.

2. Not a Priest or Counselor -- Ask Daily Faith is not a priest, counselor, or doctor.
It cannot hear your confession or give official Church guidance.
For serious spiritual, personal, or medical concerns, always talk to a real priest, adult, or professional.

3. Faithful to Catholic Teaching -- The chatbot follows Catholic teaching based on the Bible, Catechism, and tradition.
It won't discuss political topics or anything that goes against Church values.
If you ask something outside its boundaries, it may gently refuse or suggest another way to learn more.

4. Privacy & Sessions -- Your session is stored temporarily to help the chatbot remember what you've said.
It does not ask for your name, email, or personal information.
If needed, messages may be saved to help improve the experience or prevent misuse.

5. About the Creator -- This chatbot was made by William Cain, a 15-year-old Catholic student and coder.
Built using OpenAI and tools like VSCode, GitHub, and Render.
Created with the hope of helping others grow in faith.

6. Feedback & Improvements -- If you see something strange, broken, or unhelpful, please let us know!
This is a learning project and your support helps it grow.
If you have ideas for new features, questions or improvements, email me at williamtylercain441@gmail.com
